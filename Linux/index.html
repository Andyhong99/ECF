Welcome to ECF

Beyond protecting the environment, the foundation was established to provide a beautiful and clean earth for future generations of children.


Our main business can be divided into three main categories. First, Combating Desertification.The Earth is becoming more and more desertified. To prevent this, we're targeting 60 million trees a year. Second, agriculture. Agroforestry Projects aim to improve the Southeast Asia, Africa low-income farmer's planting capacity by introducing agroforestry into their farms, so they could address and overcome their community problems, such as extreme poverty, biodiversity crisis and climate changes that mainly comes from their slash&burn activities. Third, Environmental approprite technology. The demonstration project for drinking water provision and localization program by Eco Child Foundation Clean Water(ECFCW) is to relief environmental damage targeting on the Southeast Asia, Africa damaged area by typhoon Haiyan and near by poor area


We promise to be an ECF to prepare the Green Earth for our children as well as the environment through our Foundation.
